About Me

When I'm not working on my my various programming projects, I'm working on my car, playing some kind of simulator game, taking pictures, or mixing. You can check out my progress on the blog section of this site!

I love programming systems, to write code that runs silently in the backgrond. I am fastenated by the operating system kernels. One of my favorite books is,Linux Kernel Development, it completely changed the way I approach development. It taught me to approach problems with the big picture in mind while also, carefully modularizing code. I am also an avid follower of the Asahi Linux project and the Linux Kernel Mailing List.
